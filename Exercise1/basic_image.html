Basic Image Insertion




Question:

Why is the "alt" attribute important in images?

Answer:

The "alt" attribute is important because it provides a textual description of images, which helps when we look at a page and, for some reason, the image doesn't appear correctly. Additionally, it's crucial for web accessibility as it allows people with visual impairments to understand the content of the image through screen readers.
